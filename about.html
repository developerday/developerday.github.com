About Developer Day

Developer Day was born out of the realization that there's a huge amount of development talent and experience spread across the country, and there's absolutely no need to spend a lot of money to get together and learn from each other.

The first Developer Day was planned by Viget Labs and Relevance, and took place in Durham, NC in March 2009. It was a great success, and served as proof that the high-quality, low-cost local conference really worked, so the Developer Day series was born.
